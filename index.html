dSTAR Internet of Things (IoT) - is a solution for everyone who wants to take control of your home and office. It’s not only easy to install and use it, but your camera’s management is also secure with dSTAR iCommunicator.

How It Works

In order to use the product you must obtain a license from dSTAR.
First an AMI must be deployed.
After deployment discover assigned Public DNS or IPv4 from the Instance description page.
Sign up for Business account and continue to registration page.
Download dSTAR:
for Android for iOS
Get 12 digits license number and input into the dSTAR Application.
After deployment of the Instance, all features could be tested via emulation dashboard: https://<PUBLIC_IP>/testpanel
Enjoy the benefits of secure infrastructure.
